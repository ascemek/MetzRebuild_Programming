Everyone is Happy with Metz on the Table


The idea of living well is infused through the Metz experience. When you step into our dining locations, you will see it in our locally sourced ingredients, our fresh and flavorful menus, our emphasis on scratch prepared foods, and our friendly and personal service.Our goal is to create a superior program for you.


WE DO THIS BY OFFERING:

Dining concepts that reflect your tastes including ethnic foods with authentic flavors, as well as gluten-free, vegetarian and vegan options every day Sustainable, locally sourced vegetables, fruits, eggs, bread and bakery items, and poultry and livestock products within a 150-mile radius of your location Flexible dining hours along with competitive purchasing options that meet your busy schedule and needs Seasonal promotions, plus fun contests and giveaways throughout the year.

Dining With Us


We offer several dining options as a part of our work at New College. the Hamilton Café and Boar's Head Deli are our main locations, however we also offer catering for campus events. Additionally, the 4 Winds Café - a much-loved new college locations - will be opening soon. Check back for more updates! to prevent food insecurity on campus we also support the New College of Florida food pantry, a student-lead effort to gather and distribute food to NCF students facing food insecurity.
